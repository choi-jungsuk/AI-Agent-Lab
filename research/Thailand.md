# Thailand Market Research

## 1. Regulatory Environment
- **Import Regulations**: Import license required for electronics and medical devices; customs clearance via Thai Customs Department.
- **Mandatory Certifications**: Thai Industrial Standards (TIS), FDA (for food/health products), CE accepted.
- **Tariff Rates**: HS‑8 average 5‑15%; electronics 0‑5% under ASEAN‑China FTA.

## 2. Key Industries & Consumer Trends
- **Fast‑growing sectors**: Automotive components, food & beverage, renewable energy.
- **Top products**: Motorbike parts, packaged snacks, solar inverters.
- **Consumer purchasing pattern**: Strong e‑commerce growth (Shopee, Lazada); price‑sensitive but brand‑aware.

## 3. Market Price & Competition
- **Typical price range**: Motorbike parts US$10‑30 per unit; solar inverters US$0.40‑0.55 / W.
- **Major competitors**: Honda, Yamaha in automotive; CP Foods, Thai Union in food; local solar firms such as Energy Absolute.

## 4. Entry Barriers & Logistics
- **Buyer requirements**: Local distributor with Thai VAT registration, ISO 9001, after‑sales service center.
- **Logistics infrastructure**: Port of Laem Chabang (major hub), Bangkok inland transport; occasional delays due to traffic.
- **Additional notes**: Preference for CIF pricing; customs clearance 2‑4 days.
