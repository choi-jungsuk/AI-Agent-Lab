# Vietnam Market Research

## 1. Regulatory Environment
- **Import Regulations**: Requires import license for electronics; customs clearance through Vietnam Customs.
- **Mandatory Certifications**: Vietnam Standards and Quality Institute (VSQI) certification, CE marking accepted, local safety certification (Vietnamese Standard QCVN).
- **Tariff Rates**: Average HS‑8 tariff 5‑12% for consumer goods; electronics 0‑5% under ASEAN‑Australia‑New Zealand FTA.

## 2. Key Industries & Consumer Trends
- **Fast‑growing sectors**: Consumer electronics, renewable energy, agro‑food processing.
- **Top products**: Smartphones, solar panels, packaged snacks.
- **Consumer purchasing pattern**: Urban millennials favor online marketplaces (Shopee, Lazada) and value‑for‑money products.

## 3. Market Price & Competition
- **Typical price range**: Mid‑range smartphones US$150‑250; solar panels US$0.45‑0.60 / W.
- **Major competitors**: Samsung, Xiaomi, local brand Bkav in electronics; SunPower, Trina Solar in solar.

## 4. Entry Barriers & Logistics
- **Buyer requirements**: Local partner with distribution network, ISO 9001 certification, proof of after‑sales service.
- **Logistics infrastructure**: Major ports Ho Chi Minh City and Hai Phong; inland transport improving, but congestion in major cities.
- **Additional notes**: Preference for FOB pricing; customs clearance can take 3‑5 days.
