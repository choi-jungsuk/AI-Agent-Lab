# Indonesia Market Research

## 1. Regulatory Environment
- **Import Regulations**: Import license (API‑P) required for most goods; mandatory customs declaration via NPBD system.
- **Mandatory Certifications**: SNI (Indonesian National Standard), BPOM (for food/health), CE accepted.
- **Tariff Rates**: HS‑8 average 7‑15%; electronics 0‑10% depending on local content requirement.

## 2. Key Industries & Consumer Trends
- **Fast‑growing sectors**: Consumer electronics, textile & apparel, mining equipment, renewable energy.
- **Top products**: Mobile phones, garments, solar panels.
- **Consumer purchasing pattern**: Strong preference for local brands in apparel; electronics dominated by Chinese brands but growing demand for Korean tech.

## 3. Market Price & Competition
- **Typical price range**: Smartphones US$120‑200; garments US$8‑15 per piece; solar panels US$0.40‑0.50 / W.
- **Major competitors**: Oppo, Samsung in electronics; local brands like Mitra Buana in apparel; First Solar, Canadian Solar in solar.

## 4. Entry Barriers & Logistics
- **Buyer requirements**: Local distributor with NPBD registration, SNI certification, local content compliance.
- **Logistics infrastructure**: Port of Tanjung Priok (Jakarta), Surabaya; logistics costs high due to archipelago geography.
- **Additional notes**: Buyers prefer FOB terms; customs clearance 3‑7 days; regulatory compliance (SNI) critical.
